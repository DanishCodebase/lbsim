WHO WE ARE

Lal Bahadur Shastri Institute of Management (LBSIM)

Delhi is among India’s premium private business schools. LBSIM was established in 1995 to perpetuate the vision and values of Bharat Ratna Shri Lal Bahadur Shastri Ji – India’s 2nd Prime Minister, by his son - Shri Anil Shastri ji, former Union Minister and Chairman, Board of Governors. LBSIM is powered by 55+ PhD Faculty, 6000+ Alumni and 100+ Corporates.
